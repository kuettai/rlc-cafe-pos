## Opening Checklist (from SOP v8)

1. Wash hands
2. Wipe counter
3. Set out utensils
4. Ensure cups and lids sufficient for 2 services
5. Remove protective covers and place neatly on top of shelf
6. Turn on coffee machine (30 mins before use)
7. Place coffee baskets in machine, flush before use
8. Prepare: cloths for baskets, cloths for steamer, steamer mug, cups, tea, utensils
9. Ensure machine pressure at ~1 bar before use
10. Check settings for beans used (test one cup)
11. Update food items on iPad (if needed)
12. Collect food from Dream Center
13. Prepare drinks order for:
    - Chinese svc musicians (by 10:20am)
    - English svc musicians (by 10:35am)

## Closing Checklist (from SOP v8)

1. Wipe down counters, workspace
2. Wash espresso nozzles:
   - Remove coffee filter, insert blank filter (no holes)
   - Small amount of cleaning agent
   - Insert basket, run water 5-10 secs, discard dirty water
   - Repeat 5 times
   - Run again on 2nd nozzle
   - Final flush (no filter) to remove residue
3. Clean coffee machine, wipe with microfiber cloth
4. Allow machine to cool before covering
5. Dry all utensils
6. Cover: coffee machine, puck holder, coffee grinder
7. Pack away all items — leave nothing exposed
8. Keep weighing scale on flat surface (don't stack on top)
9. Check inventory → update stock count
10. Inform if items need restocking / low stock
11. Bring down to office: iPad, used cloths, leftover food, opened milk

### 1st Week RM5 → Maps to "Celebration Mode"

Our system's celebration mode should:
- Apply to black/white coffee (hot/iced) only
- NOT apply to: chocolate, chai latte, matcha, add-ons
- This is more nuanced than "all drinks RM5" — may need per-item celebration eligibility flag

## Machine Operation Notes

- Machine needs 30 min warm-up
- Flush by grinding 1 shot before use
- Grinder: left button = single shot, right button = double shot
- Top of coffee machine = cup warming only
- Coffee flow should have creamy consistency
- Dose: 16-18g per shot
- Extraction time: 25-30 seconds
- Ratio: 1:1.5 to 1:1.8 (16g coffee → ~28.8g espresso)
