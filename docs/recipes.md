# RLC Café — Drink Recipes

Extracted from church recipe document. These map to menu items and define ingredient usage for inventory tracking.

## Espresso-Based

### Latte (Hot)
- 16-18g coffee beans (espresso shot)
- ~150ml milk (frothed)

### Latte (Iced)
- 16-18g coffee beans (espresso shot)
- ~150ml milk
- Ice

### Mocha (Hot)
- 25g chocolate powder
- 30g hot water (dissolve)
- 16-18g coffee beans (espresso shot)
- ~120ml milk (frothed)

### Mocha (Iced)
- 25g chocolate powder
- 30g hot water (dissolve)
- 16-18g coffee beans (espresso shot)
- ~120ml milk
- Ice

### Citrus Black
- 20ml orange syrup (Monin Orange Fruitmix)
- 1 pump passionfruit syrup (Monin Passion Fruit)
- Soda water
- 1 espresso shot (16-18g coffee beans)
- Ice

## Non-Espresso

### Hot Chocolate
- 25g chocolate powder (63% Tiger)
- 30g hot water
- Stir with mini whisk until fully dissolved
- Froth milk slightly hotter than latte

### Iced Chocolate
- 25g chocolate powder (63% Tiger)
- 30g hot water
- Stir with mini whisk until fully dissolved
- Ice
- ~half cup milk
- Add mixed chocolate

### Butterfly Pea Soda
- 2 spoons nata de coco (ice cream scoop)
- Ice (full)
- Sprite (up to the line)
- 4-5 pumps butterfly pea tea
- 3 squeezes lemon juice (Liotti)

### Passion Fruit Soda
- 2 pumps passionfruit syrup (Monin Passion Fruit)
- Ice
- Soda water (sparkling)

### Lemon Soda
- 2 pumps lemon syrup (Monin Lemon)
- Ice
- Soda water (sparkling)

### Orange Soda
- 30ml orange syrup (Monin Orange Fruitmix)
- Ice
- Soda water (sparkling)

### Fruit Tea
- English breakfast tea, steeped 5 min (100ml)
- 2 tablespoons citron paste
- Ice
- Top up with soda water

### Ribena Tonic
- 30ml Ribena (or 0.5cm above cold cup line)
- Ice
- Tonic water

### Raspberry Iced Tea
- English breakfast tea, steeped 5 min (100ml)
- 28-30ml raspberry syrup
- Ice
- Top up with Sprite

## Notes

- "Pumps" refers to syrup bottle pump dispenser (~10ml per pump)
- Chocolate must be dissolved in hot water first with mini whisk
- Machine warm-up shot not counted as ingredient usage (waste)
- Decaf available as substitute for regular coffee beans (same quantity)
- Oat milk variant adds RM1 to price
